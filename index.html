Biography

Sam “Senior” Okret is a professor of molecular endocrinology at the department of Biosciences and Nutrition at Karolinska Institutet. His research interests are mainly focused on hormones, including estrogen and glucocorticoids. He leads the Sam Okret Group (SO-Group).
